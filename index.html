About Us

Learn more about our mission and team.

Our Mission

At Electric Eel Music Festival, our mission is to create an unparalleled experience that transcends borders and genres. We believe in the power of electronic music to unite people from all walks of life, igniting a sense of connection and euphoria that lasts long beyond the final beat. With skillful DJs, mesmerizing visuals, and a captivating atmosphere, we transport our audience to the heart of the electronic music movement.

Our Team

Meet the passionate individuals who bring Electric Eel Music Festival to life. Our team combines expertise in event organization, music curation, and technology to curate a lineup that features international musical pioneers and an electrifying mix of genres. With dedication and a shared love for electronic music, we strive to create an immersive journey that leaves a lasting impact on every attendee.
